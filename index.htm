About Us

Our Mission: To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ and build a community where believers grow in faith, serve others with love, and impact our society for the Kingdom of God.

Light Of The World Church is dedicated to worship, discipleship, and community service. We believe in the power of God's Word and the importance of fellowship. Whether you're visiting for the first time or have been with us for years, we welcome you to join our church family.
